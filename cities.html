Popular Cities

Delhi

The historical capital, Delhi is a foodies’ paradise. From street food to international cuisines, Delhi, or as we lovingly call ‘Dilli’, can blow your mind in every expanse.

Mumbai

The culture of Mumbai is a boisterous blend of cuisines, languages, food, fine arts, cinema and festivals.

Hyderbad

Traditional Hyderabadi Dum Biryani is the most celebrated dish from Hyderabad and is served at restaurants in the entire country.

Lucknow

Lucknow is a land of mouth-watering dishes, where every street and lane smells of good food.

Chennai

The main taste of Chennai lies in South Indian food mainly consists of sambhar, idli, dosa, uttapam, rasam, dry curry, and many more.

Goa

Goan cuisine consists of regional foods popular in Goa, an Indian state located along India's west coast on the shore of the Arabian Sea.

Varanasi

People in Varanasi are still like the cultural and traditional food like Jalebi and Dahi, Samosa and dahi with green chatani, Kachaudi and a variety of delicious sweets.

Agra

The most popular food item of Agra, apart from the famous Mughlai dishes, is the Pethas made from pumpkin and glucose and is popular all over India.

Jaipur

The pink city Jaipur presents to you scrumptious cuisines that are known throughout India; dishes like Dal Bati Churma, Missi Roti, and sweets like Ghevar.

Kolkata

Kolkata is known for its diversified food culture, a wide range of cuisines and varied cooking techniques. Bengali cuisine is very popular for their aromatic taste and delicious flavors.

Mirzapur

Mirzapur shares a distinctive north Indian diet. The town having Mughal ancestral root specializes in Awadhi, Mughlai as well as typical cuisines of Uttar Pradesh.

Mathura

Mathura is famous for its “Peda”, one of the famous sweet of India. Other than this sweet, Mathura is also well-known for Gol-Gappe, Jalebi – Kachori, Chat, etc.
